19
Problem

Physicians spend 2+ hours daily on documentation. It is the leading cause of burnout. One in five doctors leave medicine because of it.

But the deeper problem is health equity.

No EHR, no AI scribe, no clinical documentation tool includes any capability to detect or mitigate health disparities during the documentation process.
  • Pulse oximeters overestimate O₂ by 3.56% in Black patients (Sjoding, NEJM 2020)
  • Black women face 3–4x higher maternal mortality (CDC)
  • Implicit bias in documentation correlates with disparate outcomes (FitzGerald & Hurst, BMC Medical Ethics 2017)
  • CMS and Joint Commission mandate health equity reporting. Clinicians have zero tools.

We propose to solve both: AR glasses that eliminate the keyboard, and an AI engine that surfaces disparity alerts in real time. No one has attempted this.
