The Problem

Physicians spend 2+ hours daily on documentation—the #1 cause of burnout. One in five leave medicine. Coding errors leak ~10% of revenue. And no EHR, no AI scribe, no clinical tool addresses health disparities during documentation—despite CMS and Joint Commission mandates.

The Solution

Voice-activated AR smart glasses with an integrated health equity engine. Physicians wear lightweight glasses, speak naturally, and AI handles notes, drug checks, billing codes, and real-time health equity alerts—hands-free, into the EHR via FHIR R4.

What Makes This Novel
  • AR + Clinical Documentation: No product exists. Every competitor uses a phone or tablet.
  • Health Equity Engine: No EHR and no AI scribe includes any equity capability. We propose the first—pulse ox bias detection, maternal mortality scoring, implicit bias alerts, culturally-adapted protocols.
  • Patent Pending: US Application 15/237,980, plus ARIM and AOP (47 claims)—three filings covering the core concept.
